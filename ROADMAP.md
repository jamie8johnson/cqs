### Training Experiments — E5-base ceiling reached

Three experiments all null. E5-base + CG-filtered 200K + GIST 0.05 + 1 epoch is the ceiling.

| Experiment | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Margin sweep (Exp #2) | Null — all margins 80-83% pipeline |
